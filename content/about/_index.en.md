text = """
Anna Vershinina was born in 1992 in Ulan-Ude.

She works with etching, linocut and mixed media, exploring the interaction of time, space and human perception.

In 2020, she graduated from the Moscow State Artistic and Industrial Academy named after Stroganov, Department of Graphic Art.
Before that, she studied painting at the Irkutsk Art School.

Anna has participated in group exhibitions in Russia and abroad, including the MOI AUSSI ART GALLERY project (2020, Venice)
and Architecture of the Uncertain (2020, Moscow). Her work has also been exhibited at art fairs: Contour (2023, Nizhny Novgorod),
Art Fair 3.0 (2023, Moscow) and the Taste of Paper festival (2021, Moscow).

In her work, she explores the theme of memory, traces of time, combining real landscapes with abstract impressions and dreams.

Lives and works in Moscow.
